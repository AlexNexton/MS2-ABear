About Us

What We're About.

LingLang is an online learning platform where children learn a second language through games. Once a memeber, your child will have access to a huge variety of games that cater to their level.

They'll be assigned to a class with a teacher who will monitor their progress with regular assessment of learning goals achieved.
Lessons will be online on a weekly basis with opportunities for interactions with other children. Parents are also encouraged to participate in lessons with their child.


How We began

LingLang was started in response to Covid-19 in 2020.
We noted that children were missing out on their language education so we designed a safe space for them to continue their learning online.

LingLang's aim is quite simple - to hold children's interest by means of fun games so that they can continue learning from home, and to create a social environment online where they can make new friendships too.
